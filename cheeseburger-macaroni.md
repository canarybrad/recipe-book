# Cheeseburger Macaroni

## Ingredients
- 1 pound lean ground beef
- 1/2 cup finely diced onion (or 1 teaspoon onion powder)
- 2 cups milk
- 1 1/4 cups chicken broth
- 1 1/2 cups elbow macaroni
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 2 teaspoons garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 3/4 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese

## Instructions
1. In a small bowl, combine cornstarch, garlic powder, sugar, salt, paprika, and pepper. Set aside.
2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Brown the ground beef and onion, breaking it up with a wooden spoon, until no pink remains. Drain any excess fat.
3. Add milk, broth, and uncooked elbow macaroni to the beef. Stir in the cornstarch and spice mixture. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat.
4. Once boiling, reduce the heat to a gentle boil, cover and cook for 9-12 minutes or until the pasta is cooked through, stirring occasionally. If needed, add more broth or water.
5. Remove from heat and stir in cheese until melted. Serve hot.
