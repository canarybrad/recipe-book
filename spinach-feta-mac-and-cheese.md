# Spinach Feta Mac and Cheese

## Ingredients
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 small yellow onion, finely diced
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 fresh tomatoes, diced
- 8 to 10 ounces fresh baby spinach
- 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
- 1/2 cup cubed white cheddar cheese
- 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
- 2 cups elbow macaroni
- 1 cup vegetable broth
- 1 cup milk (dairy-free nut milk acceptable)
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
- Parsley for garnish

## Instructions
1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add onions and salt; cook 2-3 minutes until softened. Stir in garlic and cook 30 seconds until fragrant.
2. Add tomatoes, spinach, cheeses, macaroni, vegetable broth, milk, and seasonings. Mix to combine and bring to a boil, stirring frequently.
3. Lower heat to medium-low, cover with lid, and cook 12-15 minutes until pasta is cooked. Stir frequently (every 3 minutes) to prevent sticking.
4. Remove from heat and stir well. Garnish with parsley and serve immediately.
