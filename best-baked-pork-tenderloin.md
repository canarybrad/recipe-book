# Best Baked Pork Tenderloin

## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 lbs pork tenderloin (one or two tenderloins)
- 1 tablespoon oil
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 teaspoon cumin
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
- 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika (optional)
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F and lightly grease a large baking/casserole dish.
2. Pierce tenderloins all over with a fork.
3. Rub oil onto all sides of the meat.
4. Whisk together Italian seasoning, garlic powder, cumin, salt, chili powder, smoked paprika (if using), and black pepper. Sprinkle mixture over tenderloin(s), patting it onto the surface of the meat on all sides.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until cooked through, approximately 25-35 minutes depending on thickness.
