# Chicken Pot Pie

## Ingredients
- 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast halves, cubed
- 1 cup sliced carrots
- 1 cup frozen green peas
- 1/2 cup sliced celery
- 1/3 cup butter
- 1/3 cup chopped onion
- 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon celery seed
- 1 3/4 cups chicken broth
- 2/3 cup milk
- 2 (9-inch) unbaked pie crusts

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
2. In a saucepan, combine chicken, carrots, peas, and celery. Add water to cover and boil for 15 minutes. Drain and set aside.
3. In the same saucepan over medium heat, cook onions in butter until soft and translucent. Stir in flour, salt, pepper, and celery seed. Gradually mix in chicken broth and milk. Simmer over medium-low heat until thickened. Remove from heat.
4. Place the chicken and vegetable mixture in the bottom pie crust. Pour the hot liquid mixture over it.
5. Top with second crust, seal edges, trim excess dough, and cut several small slits for steam to escape.
6. Bake for 30-35 minutes until pastry is golden brown and filling is bubbly. Cool for 10 minutes before serving.
