# Turkey and Kale Pasta Bake

## Ingredients
- 3.5 cups pasta (casarecce or similar shape)
- 3 cups kale, chopped small
- 1 lb ground turkey
- 28 oz marinara sauce
- 2 cups shredded mozzarella
- 1 cup pasta water (reserved)
- 1/3 cup fresh Italian parsley
- 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1 white onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and prepare a 9x13 baking dish with spray oil or olive oil.
2. Cook pasta according to package instructions, stopping just before al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water.
3. Heat a large skillet on medium heat and add olive oil until warm.
4. Add onion and garlic, stirring until fragrant.
5. Add ground turkey and cook until almost all pink is gone.
6. Add marinara, pasta, kale, balsamic vinegar, honey, salt, and pepper. Mix well. Add pasta water and mix again.
7. In baking dish, add half the mixture and spread 1 cup shredded mozzarella evenly across it.
8. Add remaining mixture and top with remaining mozzarella.
9. Cover with foil and bake for 18 minutes or until mozzarella is melted.
