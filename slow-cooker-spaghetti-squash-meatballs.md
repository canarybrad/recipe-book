# Slow Cooker Spaghetti Squash and Meatballs

## Ingredients
- 1 medium spaghetti squash
- 1 1/2 cups crushed tomatoes
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/4 teaspoon pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
- 16 chicken meatballs
- 2 tablespoons butter or olive oil
- Additional salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Cut spaghetti squash in half crosswise and place in the bottom of a 6-quart slow cooker, cut-side down.
2. Combine tomatoes, salt, garlic powder, pepper, and oregano in a blender; puree until smooth and pour into slow cooker.
3. Place meatballs over tomatoes around spaghetti squash.
4. Cook on low for 6-7 hours or on high for 3-4 hours.
5. Remove spaghetti squash using tongs and kitchen gloves. Scoop out seeds and discard. Scoop flesh into a sieve to drain excess moisture, then transfer to a bowl and toss with butter or oil.
6. Divide between 4 plates and top with sauce and meatballs.
