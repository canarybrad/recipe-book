# Coconut Rice

## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 cups white jasmine rice
- 1 (13 oz) can unsweetened coconut milk
- 1 cup water
- 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt

## Instructions
1. Place rice in a large saucepan and add enough cold water to cover. Swirl the rice around, then drain. Repeat until water runs mostly clear.
2. Add coconut milk, 1 cup water, and salt to the rinsed rice. Stir to combine. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally.
3. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and cook until the liquid is absorbed and the rice is tender, about 15 minutes.
4. Turn off the heat and let the rice sit, covered, for 5 minutes to steam.
5. Uncover and fluff with a fork. Serve.
