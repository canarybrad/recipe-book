# Impossibly Easy Bacon Cheeseburger Pie

## Ingredients
- 6 slices bacon
- 1 lb lean ground beef (at least 80%)
- 1/4 teaspoon pepper
- 1 large onion, chopped (about 1 cup)
- 1/4 cup ketchup
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (4 oz)
- 1/2 cup Bisquick mix
- 1 cup milk
- 2 eggs

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Spray a 9-inch glass pie plate with cooking spray.
2. In a 10-inch skillet, cook bacon over medium-high heat 5 to 7 minutes, turning once, until crisp. Drain on paper towels. Crumble bacon and set aside.
3. In the same skillet, cook beef, pepper, and onion over medium-high heat 5 to 7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until beef is thoroughly cooked. Drain. Stir in ketchup.
4. Transfer beef mixture to pie plate. Top with cheese and crumbled bacon.
5. In a medium bowl, stir Bisquick, milk, and eggs with a whisk until blended. Pour into pie plate over the beef mixture.
6. Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until a knife inserted in center comes out clean. Serve with additional ketchup if desired.
