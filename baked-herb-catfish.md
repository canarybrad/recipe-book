# Baked Herb Catfish

## Ingredients
- 2 tablespoons minced fresh parsley
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 teaspoon paprika
- 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
- 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper
- 4 catfish fillets (6 ounces each)
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon butter, melted
- 1 garlic clove, minced

## Instructions
1. Combine parsley, salt, paprika, thyme, oregano, basil, and pepper in a small bowl. Sprinkle seasoning mixture over fillets on both sides.
2. Place catfish in a greased 13x9 inch baking dish. Whisk together lemon juice, butter, and garlic. Drizzle over fillets.
3. Bake uncovered at 350 degrees F for 15-20 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
