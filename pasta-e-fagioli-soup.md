# Copycat Olive Garden Pasta E Fagioli Soup

## Ingredients
- 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
- 1 lb lean ground beef
- 1 1/2 cups chopped yellow onion
- 1 cup diced carrots (about 2 medium)
- 1 cup diced celery (about 3 stalks)
- 3 cloves garlic, minced (1 tablespoon)
- 3 (8 oz) cans tomato sauce
- 2 (14.5 oz) cans low-sodium chicken broth
- 1/2 cup water (plus more as desired)
- 1 (15 oz) can diced tomatoes
- 2 teaspoons granulated sugar
- 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 3/4 teaspoon dried thyme
- 1/2 teaspoon dried marjoram
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 1 cup dry ditalini pasta
- 1 (15 oz) can dark red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 (15 oz) can great northern beans, drained and rinsed
- 3 tablespoons minced fresh parsley
- Finely shredded Romano or Parmesan cheese for serving

## Instructions
1. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Brown the ground beef, stirring occasionally until fully cooked. Drain excess fat and transfer beef to a plate.
2. Heat remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil in the same pot. Add onions, carrots, and celery and saute over medium-high heat for about 6 minutes until tender. Add garlic and cook 1 minute longer.
3. Add chicken broth, tomato sauce, water, canned tomatoes, sugar, basil, oregano, thyme, marjoram, and cooked beef. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to medium-low. Cover and simmer 15 to 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until vegetables soften.
5. Meanwhile, prepare ditalini pasta according to package directions to al dente. Drain.
6. Add cooked pasta to soup along with kidney beans and great northern beans. Thin with additional broth or water if desired.
7. Cook 1 minute longer. Stir in parsley and serve warm with grated Romano or Parmesan cheese.
