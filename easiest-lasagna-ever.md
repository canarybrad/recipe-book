# Easiest Lasagna Ever

## Ingredients
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 medium onion, diced
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
- 1 (14.5-ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
- 1 (6-ounce) can tomato paste
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
- 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
- 1 (15-ounce) container ricotta cheese
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 large egg, beaten
- 9 lasagna noodles
- 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

## Instructions
1. Heat olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add diced onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Crumble in ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.
2. Pour in crushed tomatoes, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, and red pepper flakes (if using). Season with salt and pepper. Bring to a simmer and cook for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
3. In a separate bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, and beaten egg.
4. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 baking dish. Top with 3 lasagna noodles, half the ricotta mixture, and another meat sauce layer. Repeat with noodles, ricotta mixture, and meat sauce. Top with remaining noodles and mozzarella cheese.
5. Bake in a preheated oven at 375 degrees F for 30-35 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
