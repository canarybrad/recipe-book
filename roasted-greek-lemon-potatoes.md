# Roasted Greek Lemon Potatoes

## Ingredients
- 2.5 lb potatoes (Yukon Gold recommended)
- 1 1/2 cups chicken stock/broth (low sodium)
- 1/2 cup olive oil
- 1/3 cup lemon juice
- 5 garlic cloves, finely grated
- 1 tablespoon dried oregano
- 2 teaspoons salt
- Lemon wedges, for serving
- Fresh oregano leaves, for garnish

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 390°F (200°C).
2. Peel potatoes and cut large ones into thick wedges (about 1.2 inches thick) and medium ones into 3 pieces.
3. Place potatoes in a roasting pan with all other ingredients and toss well.
4. Roast for 20 minutes. Turn potatoes and roast for a further 25-30 minutes until liquid is mostly absorbed and mainly oil remains in the pan.
5. Transfer potatoes to a separate tray. Scoop off excess oil from the original pan and drizzle over potatoes.
6. Roast for 35-40 minutes more, turning once or twice, until golden with crispy edges.
7. Return the original pan to the oven for 5-10 minutes to reduce the garlic juices.
8. Transfer potatoes to a serving platter, drizzle with reduced pan juices, and garnish with lemon wedges and fresh oregano.
