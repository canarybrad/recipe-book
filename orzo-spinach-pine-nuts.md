# Elegant Orzo with Wilted Spinach and Pine Nuts

## Ingredients
- 1 (16 oz) package uncooked orzo pasta
- 1/2 cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
- 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
- 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
- 1 cup pine nuts
- 1 (10 oz) bag baby spinach
- 1/8 cup balsamic vinegar
- 1 (8 oz) package crumbled feta cheese
- 1/2 fresh tomato, chopped
- Salt to taste

## Instructions
1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add orzo and cook for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and transfer to a mixing bowl.
2. Heat olive oil and butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Stir in garlic, basil, and red pepper flakes. Reduce heat to medium.
4. Stir in pine nuts and cook until lightly browned.
5. Add spinach, cover, and cook on low heat for 5 minutes or until spinach is wilted.
6. Combine spinach mixture with cooked orzo pasta.
7. Plate and drizzle with balsamic vinegar. Top with feta cheese and chopped tomatoes. Season with salt to taste.
