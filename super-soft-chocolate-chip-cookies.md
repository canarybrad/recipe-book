# Super Soft Chocolate Chip Cookies

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup light brown sugar
- 1 large egg
- 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 3/4 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

## Instructions
1. In a large bowl or stand mixer, combine butter and sugars until light and fluffy. Mix in egg and vanilla, then add salt, baking soda, and baking powder. Scrape bowl as needed. Incorporate flour until no streaks remain. Fold in chocolate chips by hand. Refrigerate dough for at least 1-2 hours until thoroughly chilled.
2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with a silicone mat. Scoop dough with a large cookie scoop (approximately 3 tablespoons), spacing 2-3 inches apart.
3. Bake for 12-13 minutes. Cool on pan for 3-5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
4. Store in an airtight container for up to several days or freeze for later.
