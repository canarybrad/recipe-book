# Easy Basic Bacon Quiche

## Ingredients
- 8 large eggs
- 1 cup dairy of choice (milk, half & half, or unsweetened almond milk)
- 1 deep dish pie crust
- 1 lb cooked, chopped crispy bacon
- 8 oz shredded cheddar cheese
- Salt and pepper (optional)

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
2. Whisk eggs and dairy together in a large bowl.
3. Place pie crust in a 9-inch deep dish pie plate (or use store-bought foil pan).
4. Add half the cooked bacon and half the shredded cheese to egg mixture and stir well.
5. Pour mixture into pie crust.
6. Bake at 375 degrees F for 20 minutes, then add remaining bacon and cheese on top.
7. Continue baking for 10 additional minutes until golden brown and raised.
8. Cool for about 10 minutes before serving (the quiche will settle as it cools, which is normal).
9. Cut and serve.
