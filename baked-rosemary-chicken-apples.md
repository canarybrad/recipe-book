# Baked Rosemary Chicken with Apples

## Ingredients
- 1/4 cup cider vinegar
- 2 tablespoons honey mustard
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh rosemary
- 2 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
- 1 large Honeycrisp, Fuji, or Gala apple, cored and cut into 1/2-inch wedges
- 2 large shallots, quartered
- 1/4 cup white wine

## Instructions
1. Combine vinegar, honey mustard, olive oil, garlic, salt, and rosemary in a resealable plastic bag. Add chicken, seal, and coat thoroughly with marinade. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
2. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
3. Transfer chicken and marinade to a 9x13-inch baking dish.
4. Distribute apple and shallot pieces between the chicken thighs and pour white wine over everything.
5. Bake for 1 1/2 hours until chicken is tender and golden brown.
6. Transfer chicken to a serving dish and pour pan juices over top.
