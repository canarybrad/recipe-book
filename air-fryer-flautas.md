# Air Fryer Flautas

## Ingredients
- 1 cup cooked, shredded chicken
- 1/2 cup salsa (any type or flavor)
- 1/2 cup shredded Mexican-blend cheese
- 1/4 cup cream cheese, softened
- 8 (10-inch) flour tortillas (burrito-size)
- Cooking oil spray

## Instructions
1. Combine the shredded chicken, salsa, shredded cheese, and softened cream cheese in a large bowl. Stir well until ingredients are thoroughly incorporated.
2. Heat the air fryer to 380 degrees F.
3. Place 1 to 2 tablespoons of the filling across the center of each tortilla. Roll from the bottom edge upward until completely sealed, then place seam-side down. Repeat for all eight tortillas.
4. Spray the air fryer basket with cooking oil. Arrange flautas in a single layer without crowding, seam-side down. Work in batches if necessary. Spray the tops with cooking oil.
5. Air fry for 8 to 10 minutes or until tortillas are golden brown and crispy.
6. Transfer to a serving plate and repeat with remaining batches. Serve topped with salsa, sour cream, hot sauce, or queso as desired.
