# Oatmeal Flax Blueberry Muffins

## Ingredients

### Muffins
- 1/2 cup rolled oats or old-fashioned oatmeal
- 1/2 cup buttermilk (or regular milk plus 1 teaspoon vinegar)
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salted butter, melted
- 1 egg
- 1/3 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
- 1/2 cup plus 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 heaping tablespoon ground flaxmeal
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup fresh blueberries

### Crumbly Topping and Glaze
- 1 tablespoon cold butter, cut into small pieces
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1 tablespoon brown sugar
- 1/4 cup powdered sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon milk

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 375 degrees F. Grease a muffin tin with butter and dust with flour, or use paper liners.
2. Combine oats and buttermilk in a mixing bowl. Allow to soak for 15-20 minutes. Add melted butter, egg, brown sugar, and vanilla. Stir by hand until just combined.
3. Add flour, flaxmeal, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Stir until evenly moistened.
4. Scoop a spoonful of batter into each muffin cup first. Gently fold blueberries into remaining batter and distribute evenly. Top each with 2-3 additional blueberries.
5. For the topping, press cold butter, flour, and brown sugar together with your fingers until fine crumbs form. Sprinkle over each muffin before baking.
6. Bake 13-15 minutes until golden brown. While cooling, mix powdered sugar and milk for glaze. Drizzle over cooled muffins before serving.
