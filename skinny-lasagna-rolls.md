# Skinny Lasagna Rolls

## Ingredients
- 10 whole wheat lasagna noodles, cooked al dente (about 8 minutes), well drained
- 24 oz marinara sauce (no sugar added)
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 6 cups baby spinach, chopped
- 1 cup low-fat ricotta cheese
- 1 1/2 cups part-skim mozzarella, shredded
- 1/2 cup low-fat cottage cheese
- 1 egg white
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- Kosher or sea salt, to taste
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Spread 1 1/4 cups marinara in a 13x9x2 inch casserole dish.
2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-low. Saute garlic about 1 minute until fragrant. Add spinach and cook 3 minutes until wilted.
3. In a large bowl, combine garlic, spinach, ricotta, 1 cup mozzarella, cottage cheese, egg white, oregano, salt, and pepper.
4. Lay noodles on parchment paper. Spread 1/4 cup filling on each noodle. Roll tightly from one end. Place rolls seam-side down in prepared dish. Top with 1 cup marinara, remaining mozzarella, and Parmesan.
5. Cover with foil and bake 20 minutes until cheese is hot and bubbly. Serve with additional marinara if desired.
