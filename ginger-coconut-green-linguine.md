# Ginger Coconut Green Linguine

## Ingredients
- 16 oz linguine, dry
- 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 1/2 tablespoons fresh ginger, minced
- 1 (15 oz) can lite coconut milk
- 2 teaspoons vegan sugar
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1/2 teaspoon fresh black pepper
- Red pepper flakes, to taste
- 1 bunch fresh Swiss chard, de-stemmed and chopped
- 2 large handfuls fresh spinach
- 1/4 cup fresh basil (optional)

## Instructions
1. Bring salted water to a boil and cook pasta to al dente according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
2. Heat oil in a large saute pan over low heat. Add garlic and ginger, sauteing until fragrant, 3-5 minutes.
3. Add coconut milk, sugar, lemon juice, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes. Stir well and bring to a slight simmer over medium heat.
4. Add Swiss chard and spinach. Cover and simmer 5 minutes over low heat until greens wilt.
5. Transfer mixture to a blender, add basil, and blend on high until smooth and creamy. Taste and adjust seasonings.
6. Return sauce to the saute pan and stir in cooked pasta to coat.
7. Serve hot.
