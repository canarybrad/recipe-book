# Classic Deviled Eggs

## Ingredients
- 6 large eggs
- 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
- Kosher salt and ground black pepper to taste
- Paprika for garnish

## Instructions
1. Bring water to a boil, reduce heat to low, then use a skimmer to place eggs in water. Increase heat back to high and cook for 14 minutes.
2. Remove the eggs from the water and place them in an ice water bath to cool.
3. Once cooled, peel eggs and slice lengthwise. Scoop yolks into a bowl and arrange whites on a serving plate.
4. Mash yolks with a fork, then combine with mayonnaise, mustard, vinegar, salt, and pepper until smooth.
5. Use a spoon to add a portion of the filling back into the hole of each egg white. Sprinkle with paprika for garnish.
