# Cheesecake Factory Shrimp Scampi (Copycat)

## Ingredients

### Shrimp
- 12 ounces uncooked angel hair pasta
- 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon pepper
- 1 1/2 pounds uncooked shrimp (26-30 per pound), peeled and deveined
- 3 tablespoons butter

### Sauce
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 small shallot, chopped
- 5 garlic cloves
- 1 cup dry white wine
- 2 cups heavy whipping cream
- 2 plum tomatoes, diced
- 6 fresh basil leaves, thinly sliced
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon pepper
- 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions
1. Cook angel hair pasta according to package directions. Drain.
2. Combine flour, Parmesan, salt, and pepper in a shallow dish. Add shrimp and coat, shaking off excess flour.
3. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add shrimp and cook until pink, 3-5 minutes. Remove shrimp and keep warm.
4. In the same skillet, heat olive oil and add shallot. Cook until tender, 2-3 minutes. Add garlic and cook 1 minute.
5. Add white wine and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until reduced by half, 3-4 minutes.
6. Stir in heavy cream and simmer until slightly thickened, about 5 minutes. Remove garlic cloves.
7. Add diced tomatoes, basil, salt, pepper, and Parmesan to the sauce.
8. Serve sauce in shallow dishes, top with pasta and arrange shrimp on top.
