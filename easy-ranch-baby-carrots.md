# Easy Ranch Baby Carrots

## Ingredients
- 1 (16 oz) package baby carrots, patted dry
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 1 teaspoon dried parsley
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon dried dill
- 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Prepare a baking sheet with oil or nonstick spray.
2. Combine dried parsley, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, dill, and pepper in a small bowl to create the seasoning blend.
3. Arrange carrots in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Add olive oil and the seasoning mixture, then toss gently to coat.
4. Bake for 18-20 minutes until tender.
5. Stir in butter and let it melt, approximately 1 minute.
6. Serve immediately, garnished with fresh parsley.
