# Seafood Salad

## Ingredients
- 14 ounces imitation crab
- 1/2 cup mayonnaise
- 1/2 cup finely diced celery
- 1/2 teaspoon Old Bay seasoning
- 1/2 teaspoon dill
- Salt to taste

## Instructions
1. Separate crab into individual pieces (only if compressed in package), then chop into 1/2-inch pieces. Add to a medium bowl.
2. Add remaining ingredients and gently stir until well combined.
3. Serve immediately or cover and refrigerate to store.
