# Slow Cooker Tomato Basil Parmesan Soup

## Ingredients
- 2 (14 oz) cans diced tomatoes, with juice
- 1 cup finely diced celery
- 1 cup finely diced carrots
- 1 cup finely diced onions
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano (or 1 tablespoon fresh oregano)
- 1/4 cup fresh basil
- 4 cups chicken broth
- 1/2 bay leaf
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 cups half and half, warmed (or milk for fewer calories)
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
- Cooked orzo pasta (optional)

## Instructions
1. Combine tomatoes, celery, carrots, onions, oregano, basil, chicken broth, and bay leaf in a large slow cooker.
2. Cover and cook on LOW for 5 to 7 hours (or HIGH for 3 to 4 hours) until vegetables soften and flavors blend.
3. Blend the vegetables until smooth using a blender or immersion blender. Return to slow cooker.
4. About 30 minutes before serving, melt butter over low heat in a skillet and blend in flour. Whisk constantly for 5 to 7 minutes to make a roux. Gradually whisk in hot soup, then add roux mixture back to slow cooker.
5. Add Parmesan cheese, warmed half and half, salt, and pepper to the slow cooker.
6. Cook on LOW for another 30 minutes. Serve with warm rolls, salad, and optional orzo pasta garnish.
