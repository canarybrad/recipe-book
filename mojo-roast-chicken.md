# Mojo Roast Chicken (Pollo Asado)

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
- 1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
- 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
- 2 tablespoons minced garlic
- 3 tablespoons lime juice
- 3 tablespoons orange juice
- 1 (6 lb) whole chicken, cut into pieces

## Instructions
1. Heat olive oil in a small saucepan over medium-high heat until very hot. Mash cumin, salt, oregano, and garlic into a paste in a heat-proof bowl; whisk hot oil into the paste. Allow to cool slightly. Add lime juice and orange juice; stir.
2. Place chicken in a large sealable bag; pour marinade over chicken and shake for even coating. Refrigerate at least 4 hours, up to overnight.
3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Arrange marinated chicken in a roasting pan.
4. Bake until no longer pink at the bone and juices run clear, about 1 hour. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the thickest part of the thigh near the bone should read 180 degrees F.
