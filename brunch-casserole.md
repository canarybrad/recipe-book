# Brunch Casserole

## Ingredients
- 2 boxes Betty Crocker Seasoned Skillets hash brown potatoes
- 1 1/2 lb bulk spicy pork sausage
- 2 medium red bell peppers, chopped (2 cups)
- 8 medium green onions, chopped (1/2 cup)
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese (4 oz)
- 1 cup shredded pepper Jack cheese (4 oz)
- 2 cups milk
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper
- 6 eggs
- Chopped fresh cilantro (optional)

## Instructions
1. Spray a 3-quart casserole dish with cooking spray. Cover the hash browns with 10 cups of boiling water in a 4-quart bowl. Let sit for 3 minutes, then drain thoroughly and return to the bowl.
2. In a 12-inch skillet, cook the sausage over medium heat for 5 minutes. Add bell peppers and cook for 4 additional minutes, stirring often, until the sausage is cooked through and peppers are soft. Drain excess fat. Combine the sausage mixture with potatoes in the bowl; fold in onions and half of each cheese type. Transfer to the baking dish.
3. In a separate bowl, whisk together milk, salt, pepper, and eggs. Pour this mixture over the sausage-potato base and top with remaining cheese. Cover and refrigerate for 8 hours or overnight.
4. Set oven to 375 degrees F. Uncover the dish and bake for 50 minutes until golden and cheese melts. Allow to rest 10 minutes before plating. Garnish with cilantro if desired.
