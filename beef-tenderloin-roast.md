# Perfect Beef Tenderloin Roast

## Ingredients

### Garlic Fresh Herb Butter
- 1/4 cup unsalted butter (1/2 stick), softened
- 3 to 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons fresh herbs (rosemary and thyme), finely chopped
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

### Beef
- 4 to 5 pounds beef tenderloin roast
- 2 tablespoons olive oil (or clarified butter)
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- Creamy horseradish for serving
- Fresh herbs for garnish

## Instructions
1. Remove beef from refrigerator 2 hours before cooking to bring to room temperature. Trim excess fat and silver skin with a sharp knife.
2. Tie the roast with butcher's twine at regular intervals to maintain an even shape.
3. Pat beef dry with paper towels and season generously with salt and pepper on all sides.
4. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
5. Combine softened butter, minced garlic, chopped fresh herbs, salt, and pepper in a small bowl to make the herb butter.
6. Heat an oven-safe skillet over high heat with oil or clarified butter. Sear beef for 2 to 3 minutes per side until a brown crust forms, using tongs to turn.
7. Remove from pan and rub generously with garlic herb butter on all sides.
8. Place on a wire rack set over a baking sheet. Roast at 425 degrees F for 20 to 25 minutes until internal temperature reaches desired doneness (125 degrees F for rare, 135 degrees F for medium-rare).
9. Rest 10 to 15 minutes tented with foil before slicing.
10. Slice and serve with pan juices, creamy horseradish, and fresh herbs.
