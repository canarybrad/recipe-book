# Slow Cooker Taco Soup

## Ingredients
- 2 pounds ground beef
- 1 medium onion, diced
- 2 packets ranch dressing mix
- 2 packets taco seasoning
- 32 ounces chicken broth
- 1 (14.5-ounce) can tomato sauce
- 1 (29-ounce) can diced tomatoes with chiles
- 1 (14.5-ounce) can black beans (do not drain)
- 1 (15-ounce) can corn (do not drain)
- Tortilla chips, for serving
- Sour cream, for serving
- Shredded cheddar cheese, for serving

## Instructions
1. In a large skillet, saute onions for 2 minutes.
2. Add the ground beef to the onions and brown the meat, breaking it up as it cooks.
3. Drain browned ground beef and onions to remove excess fat.
4. Add ground beef mixture and all other ingredients (do not drain corn or black beans) to the slow cooker. Stir to combine.
5. Cook on HIGH for 4 hours or LOW for 6 hours.
6. Serve with tortilla chips, sour cream, and shredded cheddar cheese.
