# Salmon with Lemon and Dill

## Ingredients
- 4 salmon fillets (skin on or off)
- Kosher salt
- Black pepper
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh dill
- 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
- 6 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
- 1/8 teaspoon kosher salt
- 2 teaspoons water

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 450 degrees F.
2. Season salmon generously with kosher salt and fresh ground black pepper. Place on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
3. Bake for about 10 minutes until flaky and just cooked through. Test doneness with a fork. Watch carefully to avoid overcooking, as timing varies by salmon thickness and oven.
4. While salmon bakes, combine dill, Greek yogurt, mayonnaise, lemon juice, garlic powder, onion powder, and salt. Add water last and mix to achieve a drizzle-able consistency.
5. Plate salmon and top each fillet with 2 tablespoons of lemon dill sauce.
