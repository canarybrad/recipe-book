# Chocolate Lasagna

## Ingredients

### Crust Layer
- 14.3 oz package of Oreos
- 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

### Cheesecake Layer
- 12 oz cream cheese, room temperature
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1/4 cup powdered sugar
- 8 oz Cool Whip, refrigerated

### Chocolate Pudding Layer
- 2 (3.9 oz) packages instant chocolate pudding
- 3 1/4 cups cold whole milk

### Top Layer
- 8 oz Cool Whip, refrigerated

## Instructions
1. Crush whole Oreos with cream in a food processor until fine crumbs form. Reserve 3 tablespoons for topping.
2. Mix crushed Oreos with melted butter. Press mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish. Refrigerate.
3. Beat cream cheese with vanilla and powdered sugar until smooth. Fold in 8 oz Cool Whip. Spread evenly over crust. Refrigerate.
4. Beat pudding mix with cold milk until thickened. Spread evenly over cheesecake layer. Refrigerate until set.
5. Spread remaining 8 oz Cool Whip over pudding layer. Sprinkle with reserved Oreo crumbs.
6. Cover and chill a minimum of 2 hours before serving.
