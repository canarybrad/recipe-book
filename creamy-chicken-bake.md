# Creamy Chicken Bake

## Ingredients
- 4 large chicken breasts
- 1 can (10 3/4 oz) condensed cream of mushroom soup
- 1 can (10 3/4 oz) condensed cream of chicken soup
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 can (8 oz) mushrooms (stems and pieces), drained
- 1/2 cup water
- 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoon chives, chopped
- 1/2 teaspoon paprika
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. Arrange chicken breasts in a 9x13-inch baking pan lightly coated with cooking spray. Season with salt and pepper.
3. In a mixing bowl, combine both cream soups, sour cream, drained mushrooms, water, onion powder, and chives. Stir until well blended.
4. Pour the creamy mixture over the chicken breasts. Sprinkle paprika on top.
5. Bake uncovered for approximately one hour until chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165 degrees F and the sauce develops a light brown surface.
6. Serve alongside rice, egg noodles, or mashed potatoes.
