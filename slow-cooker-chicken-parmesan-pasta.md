# Slow Cooker Chicken Parmesan Pasta

## Ingredients
- 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 tablespoon dried basil
- 1 teaspoon dried parsley
- 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup onion, diced
- 2 (28-ounce) cans crushed tomatoes
- 1 pound penne pasta
- 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
- Fresh parsley or basil, for garnish

## Instructions
1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper and place in the slow cooker.
2. Combine crushed tomatoes, garlic, onion, oregano, parsley, basil, and red pepper flakes in a bowl. Pour sauce mixture over the chicken.
3. Cover and cook on LOW for 4 hours or HIGH for 2 hours.
4. Remove chicken and shred with two forks.
5. Cook penne pasta on the stovetop according to package directions. Drain.
6. Add shredded chicken back to the slow cooker. Stir in the cooked pasta.
7. Top with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover and cook for 15-20 minutes until cheese is melted.
8. Garnish with fresh parsley or basil and serve.
