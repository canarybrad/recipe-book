# Pumpkin Cookies with Cream Cheese Frosting

## Ingredients

### Cookies
- 1 cup butter, softened
- 2/3 cup packed brown sugar
- 1/3 cup sugar
- 1 large egg, room temperature
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup canned pumpkin
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 cup chopped walnuts

### Frosting
- 1/4 cup butter, softened
- 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
- 2 cups confectioners' sugar
- 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Cream together butter and sugars until light and fluffy. Beat in egg and vanilla. Mix in pumpkin.
2. In a separate bowl, combine flour, cinnamon, baking soda, salt, and baking powder. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet mixture. Stir in walnuts.
3. Drop rounded tablespoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto greased baking sheets. Bake 8-10 minutes until edges are lightly browned. Cool completely on wire racks.
4. Beat frosting ingredients together until light and fluffy. Spread over cooled cookies. Store in refrigerator in an airtight container.
