# Black Bean Quinoa Salad

## Ingredients

### Dressing
- 1/4 cup lime juice (about 1 lime)
- 1/4 cup plus 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 shallot (about 3 tablespoons minced)
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/8 teaspoon black pepper

### Salad
- 1 cup uncooked quinoa
- 2 cups vegetable broth (or water)
- 2 (15 oz) cans black beans
- 1/2 cup sliced green onion
- 1 cup chopped cilantro
- 1 cup crumbled cotija cheese

## Instructions
1. Cook quinoa with 2 cups of broth according to package directions. Remove from heat and let rest for 5 minutes.
2. While quinoa cooks, combine lime juice, olive oil, minced shallot, honey, sea salt, and black pepper in a large bowl. Whisk until emulsified.
3. Drain and rinse black beans. Chop cilantro and crumble cotija cheese.
4. Fluff cooked quinoa with a fork and add to the bowl with vinaigrette. Combine with beans, cilantro, and cheese; toss until incorporated.
5. Refrigerate for at least one hour before serving.
