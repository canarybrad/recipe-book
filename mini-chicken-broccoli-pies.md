# Impossibly Easy Mini Chicken and Broccoli Pies

## Ingredients
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-size pieces
- 1 medium onion, chopped (1/2 cup)
- 1 cup frozen chopped broccoli, thawed and drained
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon pepper
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese (4 oz)
- 1/2 cup Original Bisquick mix
- 1/2 cup milk
- 2 eggs

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 375 degrees F. Spray 12 regular-size muffin cups with cooking spray.
2. In a 10-inch nonstick skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat. Cook chicken 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until no longer pink in center. Add onion and cook 2-3 minutes. Add broccoli, salt, and pepper, stirring occasionally until heated through. Cool 5 minutes, then stir in cheese.
3. In a medium bowl, whisk together Bisquick mix, milk, and eggs until blended. Spoon 1 scant tablespoon baking mixture into each muffin cup. Top with about 1/4 cup chicken-broccoli mixture. Spoon 1 tablespoon baking mixture onto chicken mixture in each cup.
4. Bake about 30 minutes until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean and tops are golden brown. Cool 5 minutes. Loosen sides with a thin knife, remove from pan, and cool 10 minutes longer before serving.
