# Asian Broccoli Salad with Peanut Sauce

## Ingredients

### Salad
- 1 large head broccoli, cut into small florets
- 1 cup shelled cooked edamame
- 1/2 cup thinly sliced green onions
- 1/2 cup peanuts
- Sesame seeds for garnish

### Peanut Sauce
- 1/4 cup natural peanut butter
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon honey or agave nectar
- 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
- 1/8 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
- 1-2 tablespoons hot water (to thin)

## Instructions
1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add broccoli florets and blanch for 1-2 minutes until bright green and crisp-tender. Drain and rinse with cold water to stop cooking.
2. Whisk together peanut butter, soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, and sesame oil until smooth. Add hot water as needed to reach desired consistency.
3. In a large bowl, combine broccoli, edamame, green onions, and peanuts. Toss with peanut sauce.
4. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve.
