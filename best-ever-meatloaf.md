# Best Ever Meatloaf

## Ingredients
- 2 lbs ground beef
- 1 cup Ritz crackers, crushed
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk
- 1 1/4 cups ketchup, divided
- 1/3 cup onion, finely chopped
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup apricot preserves
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Coat a 5x9 loaf pan with cooking spray. Finely chop the onion, crush the crackers, and beat the egg.
2. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, onion, crushed crackers, beaten egg, milk, 1/2 cup ketchup, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper. Mix with hands until combined.
3. Pour mixture into prepared pan and spread evenly. Place on a rimmed baking sheet and bake for 1 1/2 hours.
4. In a small bowl, mix together 3/4 cup ketchup, brown sugar, apricot preserves, and Worcestershire sauce.
5. Spread half the sauce mixture over the meatloaf halfway through baking.
6. Reserve remaining sauce for dipping or topping. Let meatloaf rest 15 minutes before slicing and serving.
