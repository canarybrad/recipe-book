# Chicken and Dumplings

## Ingredients

### For the Broth
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 1 large carrot, chopped
- 1 stalk celery, chopped
- 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
- 4 cups chicken broth
- 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper (or to taste)
- 2 cups shredded roasted chicken
- 1/2 cup frozen peas

### For the Dumplings
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 large egg, beaten
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons chopped chives
- 1/2 cup buttermilk (use more if needed)

## Instructions
1. Heat olive oil in a large Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add onion, carrot, and celery. Cook 5-7 minutes until onion becomes translucent. Add thyme and chicken broth.
2. Whisk flour with a ladle of broth, then pour back into the pot, whisking to remove lumps. Stir in peas and chicken. Season with salt and pepper. Cook 5 minutes.
3. While broth cooks, prepare dumplings by whisking together all dumpling ingredients. Do not overmix, as this will make them dense.
4. Drop dumpling batter by the spoonful into the broth. Make portions small since dumplings double in size while cooking.
5. Cover the pot and simmer 15-20 minutes until dumplings are cooked through. Do not lift the lid, as steam is necessary for fluffy dumplings. Test doneness with a toothpick after 15 minutes.
