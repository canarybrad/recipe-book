# Simple Crock Pot Chicken

## Ingredients
- 4 to 6 chicken breasts (bone-in or boneless)
- 1 can (14.5 oz) chicken broth (plus water if needed to cover chicken)
- 2 packets chicken gravy mix
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Place chicken breasts in the crock pot. Season with salt and pepper.
2. Pour chicken broth over the chicken. If it does not cover the chicken, add a little water.
3. Cook on low for 8 hours or on high for 4-5 hours. Chicken is done when it falls off the bone or shreds easily with a fork.
4. Remove chicken from the crock pot.
5. Pour 2 cups of broth from the crock pot into a saucepan. Add 2 packets of gravy mix and stir over medium heat until thickened, about 2 minutes.
6. Serve gravy over chicken with mashed potatoes or noodles.
