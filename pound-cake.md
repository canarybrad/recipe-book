# Pound Cake

## Ingredients
- 3 cups all-purpose flour (360g)
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature (226g)
- 3 cups granulated sugar (600g)
- 8 large eggs, room temperature
- 1 cup sour cream, room temperature (240g)
- 4 teaspoons vanilla extract

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Butter and flour a 12-cup bundt pan or grease with baking spray, making sure to coat every crevice.
2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, salt, and baking powder, then set aside.
3. In a stand mixer fitted with a whisk attachment, beat the butter on medium speed for about 2 minutes until creamy. Add the sugar and mix for an additional 2 minutes, until combined. Scrape the bowl down and beat a few more seconds.
4. While mixing at medium-low speed, add the eggs one at a time. Scrape the bowl down and beat until fully incorporated.
5. Add the sour cream and vanilla. Mix until incorporated. Scrape the bowl down.
6. Add the flour mixture and mix on low until combined. Scrape the bowl down and use your spatula to mix in any pockets of flour or butter that remain.
7. Transfer the batter to the prepared bundt pan and spread evenly. Bake for about 1 hour and 15 minutes. Start checking after 1 hour — the cake is done when the top is golden and a skewer inserted into the center comes out clean.
8. Let the cake cool in the pan for 15 minutes, then invert onto a cooling rack and cool completely before slicing.
