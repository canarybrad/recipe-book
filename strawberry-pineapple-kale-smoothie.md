# Strawberry Pineapple Kale Smoothie

## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 cups strawberries
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
- 1 cup chopped kale, ribs and thick stems removed
- 1/2 cup chopped pineapple
- 1 banana
- 1/2 cup apple juice
- 1 cup ice (divided)

## Instructions
1. Combine strawberries, milk, honey, and 1/2 cup ice in a blender until smooth. Set aside.
2. Combine kale, pineapple, banana, apple juice, and 1/2 cup ice in blender until smooth. Set aside.
3. Serve strawberry mixture immediately, topped with kale mixture.
