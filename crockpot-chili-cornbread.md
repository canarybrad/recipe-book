# Crockpot Chili with Cornbread

## Ingredients

### For the Chili
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 stalk celery, chopped
- 1 small onion, finely diced
- 1/2 tablespoon minced garlic
- 1 (15-ounce) can petite diced tomatoes
- 1 (15-ounce) can tomato sauce
- 1 (15-ounce) can dark red kidney beans
- 1 (15-ounce) can beef broth
- 1 (15-ounce) can refried beans
- 1 1/2 tablespoons chili powder
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

### For Serving
- Cornbread (prepared from your preferred recipe or mix)
- Shredded cheddar cheese
- Sour cream

## Instructions
1. Brown and crumble the ground beef with celery, onion, and garlic in a medium skillet over medium-high heat until the meat is completely browned and vegetables are tender. Drain excess grease.
2. Pour the beef mixture into a 4-quart or larger crock pot.
3. Add the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, kidney beans, beef broth, and refried beans to the crock pot. Break up the refried beans and stir to combine.
4. Add chili powder, cumin, and Worcestershire sauce. Mix thoroughly.
5. Cover and cook on LOW for 4-6 hours (the longer, the better).
6. Serve over cornbread with shredded cheese and sour cream.
