# Easy Garlic Chicken

## Ingredients
- 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 4 tablespoons brown sugar
- 3 teaspoons olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Spray a casserole dish with cooking spray or line with aluminum foil.
2. In a small pan, saute minced garlic in olive oil until translucent but not browned. Remove from heat.
3. Place chicken breasts in the casserole dish and sprinkle with salt and pepper on both sides.
4. Mix brown sugar into the sauteed garlic and distribute evenly on top of the chicken breasts.
5. Bake uncovered for 15 to 20 minutes or until chicken is cooked through and the garlic and sugar have melted into a sweet glaze.
