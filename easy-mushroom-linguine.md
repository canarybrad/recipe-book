# Easy Mushroom Linguine

## Ingredients
- 160 g (5.5 oz) linguine
- 25 g (2 tablespoons) butter
- 250 g (9 oz) chestnut mushrooms, sliced
- 4 cloves garlic, peeled and crushed
- 4 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
- Pinch of chili flakes
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- 40 g (1.5 oz) Parmesan cheese, grated
- Handful fresh parsley, finely chopped
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Cook the linguine according to package directions.
2. Heat butter in a frying pan and cook the sliced mushrooms until soft, golden, and liquid has mostly evaporated.
3. Add garlic, thyme, and chili flakes; cook for a couple more minutes. Season well.
4. Drain the linguine, reserving one cup of cooking water.
5. Combine linguine with mushroom mixture, lemon juice, Parmesan, and parsley. Toss together and add enough reserved water to form a light sauce.
6. Adjust seasoning and serve.
