# Red Curry Chicken Wings

## Ingredients
- 3 lbs chicken wing pieces (tips discarded)
- 1 (14 oz) can unsweetened coconut milk
- 2 tablespoons red curry paste
- 2 tablespoons fish sauce
- 1 medium onion, cut into thin wedges
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 2 tablespoons cold water
- 1/4 cup fresh basil, slivered (optional)

## Instructions
1. In a 3 1/2- or 4-quart slow cooker, combine coconut milk, fish sauce, and curry paste. Add wing pieces and onion, stirring to coat with curry mixture.
2. Cover and cook on low-heat setting for 3 to 4 hours or on high-heat setting for 1 1/2 to 2 hours.
3. Using a slotted spoon, remove chicken from cooker; cover with foil to keep warm.
4. Skim fat from curry mixture. In a medium saucepan, combine cornstarch and water; stir in curry mixture. Cook and stir over medium heat until thickened and bubbly. Cook and stir for 2 minutes more.
5. Stir in basil if desired. Serve chicken wings with sauce.
