# Philly Cheese Potatoes

## Ingredients
- 3 lb Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and cut into small chunks
- 1 can (14.5 oz) fat-free reduced-sodium chicken broth
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tub (8 oz) Philadelphia Cream Cheese Spread

## Instructions
1. Cook potatoes in boiling water in a large saucepan for 15 minutes or until tender. Drain.
2. Mix broth and seasonings in a microwaveable bowl until blended. Microwave on high for 1 1/2 minutes or until heated through.
3. Return potatoes to the saucepan. Add broth mixture.
4. Mash potatoes to desired consistency.
5. Add cream cheese and stir until completely melted and mashed potatoes are well blended.
6. Serve as a side dish with your favorite meat, poultry, or fish.
