# Simple Oven Baked Brisket

## Ingredients
- 4 lb beef brisket, trimmed with a thin layer of fat remaining
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 2 tablespoons paprika
- 2 1/2 cups beef broth
- 1/4 cup sharp mustard (optional, for serving)
- 1/2 cup prepared horseradish sour cream sauce (optional, for serving)

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F. Prepare a roasting pan with a rack.
2. Combine salt, pepper, onion powder, garlic powder, and paprika in a small bowl. Rub this seasoning mixture on all sides of the brisket.
3. Place brisket fat-side up on the roasting rack in the pan. Add enough beef broth to cover the bottom of the pan by approximately half an inch.
4. Cover tightly with aluminum foil (use two pieces for a secure seal) or a lid. Bake for 1 hour.
5. Lower temperature to 275 degrees F and continue cooking, covered, for an additional 3 hours or until fork-tender. Monitor liquid level periodically and add more broth if needed.
6. Cool approximately 15 minutes while covered, then slice thinly. Trim any excess fat from the top if desired.
7. Serve with horseradish sour cream and/or sharp mustard. Drizzle or soak slices in the leftover pan sauce to maintain moisture.
