# 3 Ingredient Crockpot Chicken Tacos

## Ingredients
- 6 boneless skinless chicken breasts (frozen or thawed)
- 1 jar (16 oz) salsa
- 1 packet (1.25 oz) taco seasoning
- 12 taco shells or flour tortillas

### Optional Toppings
- 1 cup guacamole
- 2 cups shredded lettuce
- 1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
- 2 diced tomatoes
- 1 can (6 oz) sliced olives
- 1 cup sour cream

## Instructions
1. Spray slow cooker with non-stick cooking spray.
2. Place chicken breasts at the bottom of the slow cooker.
3. Pour salsa over the chicken and sprinkle taco seasoning on top.
4. Cook on high for 4 to 6 hours or on low for 6 to 8 hours.
5. When chicken is done, shred with two forks directly in the slow cooker and stir to coat in sauce.
6. Serve shredded chicken in taco shells or flour tortillas with desired toppings.
