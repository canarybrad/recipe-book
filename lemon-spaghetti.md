# Lemon Spaghetti

## Ingredients
- 1 pound thin spaghetti
- 1/2 cup olive oil
- 2/3 cup Parmesan cheese, shredded
- 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice (2-3 lemons)
- 2 tablespoons lemon zest, divided
- 1/4 cup fresh Italian parsley, chopped
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Instructions
1. Cook the pasta in salted water per package directions until al dente.
2. While pasta cooks, whisk together olive oil, Parmesan, 1 tablespoon of the lemon zest, and lemon juice in a large serving bowl.
3. Drain pasta and transfer to the serving bowl with lemon sauce. Toss to coat.
4. Add pepper and salt, then toss. Adjust seasoning to taste.
5. Add chopped parsley and toss. Top with remaining lemon zest.
6. Serve warm.
