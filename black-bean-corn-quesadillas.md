# Black Bean and Corn Quesadillas

## Ingredients
- 1 teaspoon olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1/4 small onion, chopped
- 1/4 bell pepper (any color), chopped
- 1 (15 oz) can black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 heaping cup corn (canned or frozen, thawed)
- 1/4 cup salsa
- 1 teaspoon light brown sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon cumin
- 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
- 2 cups shredded cheese (Monterey Jack, Colby Jack, or cheddar)
- 4 large flour tortillas
- Sour cream, for serving

## Instructions
1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Saute onion and bell pepper for 1-2 minutes.
3. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds. Mix in black beans, corn, salsa, brown sugar, cumin, and chili powder.
4. Reduce heat and adjust seasonings to taste.
5. Heat another skillet. Place one tortilla inside and sprinkle cheese, then add a heaping scoop of filling spread evenly.
6. Top with more cheese and place another tortilla on top. Cook until the bottom tortilla turns golden.
7. Flip carefully using a large spatula. Cook until cheese melts and bottom is golden brown.
8. Serve with sour cream and salsa.
