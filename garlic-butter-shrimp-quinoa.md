# Garlic Butter Shrimp and Quinoa

## Ingredients
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
- 5 teaspoons minced garlic, divided
- 2 cups uncooked quinoa
- 1 teaspoon chili powder, divided
- 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
- 6 tablespoons salted butter, divided
- 1 pound raw tail-on shrimp
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley for serving
- Fresh lemon juice for serving

## Instructions
1. Heat oil in a large nonstick pot over medium-high heat. Add onion and saute until softened, about 5 minutes. Add 2 teaspoons garlic and saute for 1 minute, stirring constantly. Add uncooked quinoa and 1/2 teaspoon chili powder. Season with salt and pepper. Saute for 1 minute. Add broth, bring to a boil, cover and cook 15-20 minutes until quinoa is soft. Fluff with a fork and toss with fresh parsley.
2. While quinoa cooks, heat 1 tablespoon butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add shrimp, sprinkle with remaining 1/2 teaspoon chili powder, and season with salt and pepper. Saute until no longer translucent and golden brown. Add 1 teaspoon garlic at the end and swirl until fragrant.
3. Melt remaining 5 tablespoons butter with 2 teaspoons garlic to create a drizzling sauce.
4. Serve quinoa and shrimp together. Top with fresh parsley and lemon juice if desired. Drizzle butter sauce over top and serve immediately while hot.
