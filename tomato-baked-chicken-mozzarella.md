# Garlic Butter Tomato Baked Chicken with Mozzarella

## Ingredients

### Chicken Marinade
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

### Balsamic Tomato Pan Sauce
- 3 cups halved cherry tomatoes
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons chopped basil
- 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
- 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

## Instructions
1. Combine olive oil, white wine vinegar, minced garlic, red pepper flakes, and Italian seasoning in a zip-top bag. Add chicken breasts, season with salt and pepper, seal, and massage to coat evenly. Let sit 10 to 15 minutes.
2. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
3. In an oven-safe baking dish, combine cherry tomatoes, balsamic vinegar, melted butter, honey, garlic, basil, onion powder, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Toss to combine.
4. Arrange tomatoes around the edges of the dish and place marinated chicken breasts in the center.
5. Bake uncovered for 18 to 22 minutes or until the chicken is almost cooked through.
6. Top chicken with shredded mozzarella and broil briefly until cheese is melted and bubbly.
7. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley. Serve with bread, pasta, rice, or vegetables.
