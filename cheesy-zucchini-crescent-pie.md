# Cheesy Zucchini Crescent Pie

## Ingredients
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 cups thinly sliced zucchini
- 1 cup chopped onions
- 2 tablespoons dried parsley flakes
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/4 teaspoon dried basil leaves
- 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano leaves
- 2 eggs, well beaten
- 2 cups shredded Muenster or mozzarella cheese (8 oz)
- 1 can (8 oz) refrigerated Pillsbury crescent rolls (8 count)
- 2 teaspoons yellow mustard

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 375 degrees F.
2. In a 12-inch skillet, melt butter over medium-high heat. Add zucchini and onions and cook 6 to 8 minutes, stirring occasionally, until tender. Stir in parsley flakes, salt, pepper, garlic powder, basil, and oregano.
3. In a large bowl, mix eggs and cheese. Add cooked vegetable mixture and stir gently to combine.
4. Separate crescent dough into 8 triangles. Place in an ungreased 10-inch glass pie plate, 12x8-inch (2-quart) glass baking dish, or 11-inch quiche pan. Press over bottom and up sides to form a crust. Firmly press perforations to seal.
5. Spread crust with mustard. Pour egg mixture evenly into crust-lined pie plate.
6. Bake 18 to 22 minutes or until a knife inserted near center comes out clean. If necessary, cover edge of crust with strips of foil during last 10 minutes of baking to prevent excessive browning.
7. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
