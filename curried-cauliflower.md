# Curried Cauliflower

## Ingredients
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1/3 cup olive oil
- 1 1/2 teaspoons curry powder
- 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 large head cauliflower, cored and cut into florets
- 1/2 red onion, peeled and sliced
- 1/3 cup slivered roasted almonds
- 1/4 cup golden raisins
- 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 425 degrees F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. In a large bowl, whisk together lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, curry powder, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Add cauliflower, red onion, and almonds; toss to coat.
3. Spread mixture in a single layer on prepared baking sheet and bake for 25-30 minutes until cauliflower reaches desired tenderness.
4. Stir in raisins, garnish with fresh cilantro, and serve.
