# Santa Monica Street Tacos

## Ingredients

### For the Tacos
- 1 teaspoon olive oil
- 1 lb skirt or flank steak, trimmed of fat and sliced into thin strips
- 1 small onion, finely diced
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 6-8 (six inch) white corn tortillas, warmed
- 1/4 cup chopped cilantro leaves
- 2 cups thinly sliced red cabbage

### For the Sauce
- 1/2 cup cilantro leaves
- Juice of 2 limes
- 1/3 cup olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, pressed
- 2 teaspoons white vinegar
- 1/2 teaspoon honey
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1/2 cup sour cream
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add beef and onion and reduce to medium. Season with salt, pepper, cayenne, and garlic powder. Cook 8-10 minutes until no longer pink. Add minced garlic and cook 1 minute. Remove from heat.
2. While beef cooks, combine all sauce ingredients in a food processor and pulse until smooth.
3. Assemble tacos by adding beef to warm tortillas along with cilantro and cabbage. Top with sauce and serve.
