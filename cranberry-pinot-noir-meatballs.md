# Cranberry Pinot Noir Meatballs

## Ingredients

### Meatballs
- 2 pounds extra-lean ground beef
- 1 1/2 cups fresh homemade bread crumbs
- 1/2 cup celery, finely chopped
- 1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- 2 teaspoons garlic salt
- 1/4 teaspoon pepper

### Cranberry Pinot Noir Sauce
- 1 (16-ounce) can whole cranberry sauce
- 1 cup brown sugar, firmly packed
- 1 cup Pinot Noir wine (any red wine may be substituted)
- 2 teaspoons hot Chinese mustard

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Lightly oil a large shallow baking dish.
2. Prepare sauce by combining cranberry sauce, brown sugar, wine, and mustard in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Stir to combine and simmer 5 minutes. Remove from heat.
3. Combine ground beef, bread crumbs, celery, onion, eggs, Worcestershire sauce, garlic salt, and pepper. Mix thoroughly using hands. Shape into 1-inch balls and place in a single layer in prepared pan.
4. Bake 20 minutes, turning once. Drain grease.
5. Transfer meatballs to a heated chafing dish or slow cooker. Pour prepared sauce over meatballs and keep warm on low heat while serving. Serve with appetizer toothpicks or skewers.
