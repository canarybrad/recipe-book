# Crockpot BBQ Chicken

## Ingredients
- 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- 1 cup BBQ sauce
- 1/4 cup Italian dressing
- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- Salt to taste

## Instructions
1. Season chicken breasts lightly with salt and place in the crockpot.
2. In a mixing bowl, combine BBQ sauce, Italian dressing, brown sugar, and Worcestershire sauce. Stir until well combined.
3. Pour sauce over chicken. Cover and cook on HIGH for 3-4 hours.
4. Serve the breasts whole, or shred with two forks. If shredding, re-cover and let cook in sauce for 10-15 more minutes to absorb the flavors.
5. Serve on buns, over rice, in wraps, on a salad, or topped with coleslaw.
