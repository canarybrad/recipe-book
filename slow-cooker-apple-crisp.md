# Slow Cooker Apple Crisp

## Ingredients

### Filling
- 6 medium apples (about 2 lbs), peeled, cored, and sliced into 1/2-inch slices
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

### Topping
- 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup old-fashioned oats
- 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup cold salted butter, cut into pieces

### Optional
- Vanilla ice cream for serving

## Instructions
1. In a large bowl, combine apple slices with granulated sugar, 2 tablespoons flour, and cinnamon until evenly coated.
2. Spray a 4- to 5-quart slow cooker with cooking spray. Distribute the apple mixture in an even layer.
3. In a medium bowl, mix 1/2 cup flour, oats, brown sugar, and salt. Using a fork or pastry blender, cut cold butter pieces into the mixture until crumbly. Do not overmix.
4. Distribute topping evenly over the apple mixture.
5. Cover and cook on High for 2 hours. Turn off heat, remove cover, and cool 30 minutes.
6. Serve warm with vanilla ice cream if desired.
