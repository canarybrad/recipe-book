# Creamy Steak 'n Onion Dip

## Ingredients
- 3/4 cup Greek yogurt (or sour cream)
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 1 1/2 teaspoons Montreal Steak seasoning
- 2 to 2 1/2 teaspoons 3 Onion Dip Mix (such as Epicure brand)

## Instructions
1. Combine Greek yogurt, mayonnaise, steak seasoning, and onion dip mix in a bowl.
2. Mix well until all ingredients are fully incorporated.
3. Serve with chips, sliced veggies, or use as a spread on burgers and sandwiches.
