# Grilled Chicken Skewers with Peanut Sauce

## Ingredients

### Marinade
- 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 12 strips
- 1/2 cup lite soy sauce (or tamari)
- 2 tablespoons sesame oil
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 teaspoons fresh grated ginger
- 2 teaspoons red pepper flakes

### Peanut Sauce
- 1/2 cup natural peanut butter
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 2 tablespoons lite soy sauce (or tamari)
- 2 teaspoons fresh grated ginger
- 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 1/4 cup water

## Instructions
1. Combine soy sauce, sesame oil, honey, vinegar, garlic, ginger, and red pepper flakes. Submerge chicken strips in marinade, refrigerate for at least 5 hours or overnight.
2. Blend all peanut sauce ingredients until smooth, adjusting water as needed. Refrigerate until serving.
3. Thread marinated chicken onto skewers and chill. Heat remaining marinade in saucepan until thickened.
4. Oil grill grates and heat to medium-high. Cook skewers 3-4 minutes per side until cooked through, basting frequently.
5. Serve with peanut sauce.
