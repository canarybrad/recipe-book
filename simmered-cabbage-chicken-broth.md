# Simmered Cabbage in Chicken Broth

## Ingredients
- 1/2 head cabbage, chopped into 1-inch squares
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon pepper
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 2 teaspoons sugar (optional)

## Instructions
1. Heat oil and butter in a large pot until melted.
2. Thoroughly wash and rinse cabbage several times to remove dirt.
3. Add chopped cabbage, salt, and pepper, stirring over low heat for 5 minutes.
4. Pour in the broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and cook for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until tender.
5. Uncover, raise heat to medium, and cook until liquids reduce by half.
6. Taste and season as needed.
