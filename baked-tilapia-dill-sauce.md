# Baked Tilapia with Dill Sauce

## Ingredients

### Tilapia
- 4 tilapia fillets
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1 lemon, sliced

### Dill Sauce
- 1/2 cup mayonnaise
- 1/2 cup sour cream
- 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
2. Pat tilapia fillets dry with a paper towel, then drizzle with olive oil and rub evenly.
3. Sprinkle salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and paprika on both sides of fillets.
4. Place seasoned fillets in a single layer in a baking dish and arrange lemon slices on top.
5. Bake for 15-20 minutes until fish flakes easily with a fork.
6. Meanwhile, combine mayonnaise, sour cream, fresh dill, lemon juice, and garlic powder in a bowl. Mix until well combined and season with salt and pepper to taste. Refrigerate for at least 15 minutes before serving.
7. Plate tilapia and drizzle with chilled dill sauce.
