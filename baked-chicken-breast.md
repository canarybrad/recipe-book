# Juicy Baked Chicken Breast

## Ingredients
- 2 lb boneless skinless chicken breast (about 4 medium pieces)
- 1 1/2 tsp fine sea salt
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
- 1 tablespoon olive oil, avocado oil, or melted butter

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Pound chicken breasts between plastic wrap to an even thickness (about 3/4 inch).
3. Pat chicken dry with paper towels, then brush all sides with oil or butter.
4. Combine salt, garlic powder, paprika, and black pepper in a small bowl. Sprinkle mixture over both sides of chicken.
5. Arrange chicken on the baking sheet with space between pieces.
6. Bake at 400°F for 18-22 minutes (adjust based on thickness) until internal temperature reaches 165°F.
7. Rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing and serving.

### Bake Time by Thickness
- 1/2 inch thick: 16-18 minutes
- 3/4 inch thick: 20-22 minutes
- 1 inch thick: 24-26 minutes
