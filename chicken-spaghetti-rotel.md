# Chicken Spaghetti with Rotel Tomatoes

## Ingredients
- 3 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 1 (10.75 oz) can cream of chicken soup
- 1 (10 oz) can Rotel diced tomatoes with green chilies (do not drain)
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 8 ounces Velveeta cheese, cut into cubes
- 2 ounces cream cheese
- 8 ounces spaghetti
- 1 cup cheddar cheese, grated
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions for al dente. Drain.
3. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium to medium-high heat. Add chicken pieces, sprinkle with onion powder, and add garlic. Saute for 7-10 minutes until chicken is no longer pink.
4. Pour in the cream of chicken soup, cubed Velveeta, cubed cream cheese, and Rotel tomatoes. Reduce heat to low and cook while stirring constantly until cheese melts.
5. Add drained spaghetti and stir into the cheese and chicken mixture until fully coated. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
6. Lightly grease a 2-quart casserole dish and pour in the mixture. Sprinkle cheddar cheese on top.
7. Bake for 25-30 minutes until heated through and cheese is melted.
