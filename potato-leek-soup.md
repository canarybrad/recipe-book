# Potato Leek Soup (Creamy Without Cream)

## Ingredients
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 3 large leeks, dark green tops and roots removed, halved lengthwise and sliced crosswise into 1/4-inch pieces
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 4 cups vegetable broth (or more for thinner texture)
- 2 pounds Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and diced into 1/2-inch pieces
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 sprigs fresh thyme
- Freshly chopped chives for garnish
- Black pepper for garnish

## Instructions
1. Place chopped leeks in a colander and rinse thoroughly under cold water to remove dirt.
2. Heat olive oil in a Dutch oven over medium heat. Add leeks and cook 8 to 10 minutes until softened, avoiding browning. Add garlic and cook 1 minute more.
3. Add diced potatoes, salt, bay leaf, thyme sprigs, and vegetable broth. Increase heat to high and bring to a boil, then reduce to low. Cover and simmer 15 to 20 minutes until potatoes are fork-tender.
4. Remove and discard bay leaf and thyme sprigs.
5. Use an immersion blender to blend to desired creaminess, or blend in batches using a high-powered blender.
6. Serve topped with chopped chives and freshly ground black pepper.
