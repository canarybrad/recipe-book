# Simple Baked Salmon with Dill

## Ingredients
- 2 pounds salmon
- 2 medium lemons
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 3 tablespoons fresh chopped dill, divided
- Fresh ground pepper

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
2. Line a sheet pan with foil and coat lightly with cooking spray. Place the salmon on the foil.
3. Drizzle the olive oil over the salmon and rub to coat. Spread the minced garlic evenly on top, then sprinkle with salt, pepper, and half of the fresh dill. Place thin slices of lemon on top of the salmon.
4. Fold the foil up and around the salmon, crumpling or folding the pieces together to seal. Fold up the ends to form a large sealed pouch.
5. Bake for around 15 minutes. Remove from oven and change oven setting to broil.
6. Carefully open the pouch and fold the foil back over the edges of the sheet pan. Move the lemons off the salmon and place around the sides.
7. Return the salmon to the oven and broil for around 3 minutes until the top begins to brown and the edges begin to crisp.
8. Remove from oven, top with the remaining dill, and serve immediately.
