# Seven Layer Christmas Salad

## Ingredients

### Salad
- 5 cups romaine or iceberg lettuce, chopped
- 1 large red onion, finely chopped
- 2 cups apples, chopped
- 2 cups dried cranberries, roughly chopped
- 6 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
- 1 cup gorgonzola cheese, crumbled

### Candied Nuts
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1 tablespoon butter

### Dressing
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 tablespoon dijon mustard
- 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
- 2 tablespoons maple syrup
- Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add walnuts, sugar, and butter. Stir frequently for 5 minutes. Once sugar begins melting, stir constantly.
2. When all sugar has melted and nuts are coated, transfer to parchment paper and break nuts apart with a spatula. Let rest 5 to 7 minutes.
3. In a large glass bowl, layer lettuce on the bottom.
4. Add layers of red onion, apples, and cranberries in sequence.
5. Whisk together mayo, sour cream, maple syrup, mustard, and vinegar. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Pour dressing over salad and smooth with a spatula. Cover and chill up to 2 days.
7. When ready to serve, sprinkle gorgonzola, bacon, and candied nuts on top. Toss and serve.
