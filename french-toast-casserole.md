# French Toast Casserole (Make Ahead)

## Ingredients

### Casserole
- 1 pound loaf of brioche, challah, French bread, or white bread
- 8 large eggs
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 2 cups milk
- 1/4 cup light brown sugar
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

### Crumb Topping
- 3 tablespoons light brown sugar, packed
- 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 2 tablespoons cold butter, cut into pieces

## Instructions
1. Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish. Cut bread into 1-inch chunks and place in the pan.
2. Whisk eggs in a large mixing bowl. Add cream, milk, brown sugar, cinnamon, salt, and vanilla. Mix well.
3. Pour egg mixture evenly over the bread cubes and toss gently to combine. Spread into an even layer.
4. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
5. Make the crumb topping: combine brown sugar, white sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg in a bowl. Add cold butter and use a pastry blender or your hands to combine until crumbly. Cover and refrigerate until morning.
6. In the morning, preheat oven to 350°F.
7. Uncover the casserole and sprinkle the crumb topping evenly over the top.
8. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until the top is golden and the casserole is set.
9. Allow to cool for at least 10 minutes before serving. Top with maple syrup, berries, or whipped cream.
