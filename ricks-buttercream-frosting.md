# Rick's Special Buttercream Frosting

## Ingredients
- 2 cups shortening
- 8 cups confectioners' sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 cup heavy whipping cream

## Instructions
1. In a mixing bowl, cream shortening until fluffy. Add sugar and continue creaming until well blended.
2. Add salt, vanilla, and 6 ounces of the whipping cream. Blend on low speed until moistened.
3. Add additional 2 ounces whipping cream if necessary. Beat at high speed until frosting is fluffy.
