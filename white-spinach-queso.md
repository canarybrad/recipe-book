# White Spinach Queso

## Ingredients
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1/2 cup diced white onion
- 1 jalapeno, seeded and diced
- 12 ounces shredded white American cheese
- 4 ounces shredded pepper jack cheese
- 2/3 cup half and half
- 1 (15-ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chiles, drained
- 2 cups chopped fresh spinach
- 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro

## Instructions
1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add diced onion and jalapeno, saute until tender.
2. Stir in white American cheese, pepper jack cheese, and half and half until melted and smooth.
3. Add drained tomatoes with green chiles, fresh spinach, and cilantro. Stir well.
4. Transfer to a serving dish and serve with tortilla chips.
