# Sriracha Quesarito

## Ingredients

### Main
- Large flour tortillas
- Shredded cheddar cheese
- 1 pound ground meat
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- Kosher salt
- Cilantro lime rice
- Sour cream

### Taco Seasoning
- 1/2 tablespoon flour
- 1/2 tablespoon cornstarch
- 2 teaspoons onion powder
- 2 teaspoons garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- 1/2 teaspoon cumin
- 1/2 teaspoon granulated sugar

### Sriracha Cream Sauce
- 1/4 cup sour cream
- 1/4 cup mayonnaise
- 1 1/2 tablespoons Sriracha
- 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon cumin
- 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
- 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
- Pinch of kosher salt

## Instructions
1. Mix all taco seasoning ingredients together and set aside.
2. Combine all Sriracha cream sauce ingredients, adjusting Sriracha to taste. Set aside.
3. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Brown ground meat, breaking into small pieces. Season with salt. Add taco seasoning and 1/2 cup water. Simmer 6-8 minutes until water evaporates.
4. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Layer cheese between two tortillas on a foil-lined baking sheet. Seal with foil and bake 5 minutes until cheese melts.
5. Remove foil and top with rice, beef, Sriracha sauce, and sour cream. Roll into a burrito.
