# Turkey Burgers

## Ingredients
- 1 lb ground turkey meat (thigh meat preferred)
- 1 medium red onion, finely chopped
- 1/2 cup fresh parsley, minced (or other fresh herbs)
- 1 tbsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp pepper
- Cheese slices (optional)

## Instructions
1. Combine all ingredients and mix together to form 4-6 patties. Make patties flat and wide because they will shrink to the size of the bun.
2. Grill on medium heat for 7 minutes per side.
3. Two minutes before the burgers finish cooking, place a cheese slice on each patty if desired.
4. Serve hot on potato roll buns with lettuce, tomato, pickles, ketchup, and mayonnaise.
