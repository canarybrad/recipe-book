# Slow Cooker Honey Lime Ginger Pork

## Ingredients
- 2.5 pounds pork loin
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1/2 cup honey
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- 2 teaspoons lime juice (about 1 lime)
- 2 teaspoons minced garlic
- 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger (or 1 tablespoon peeled and minced fresh ginger)
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- Chopped cilantro, for garnish
- Lime wedges, for garnish

## Instructions
1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Season the pork loin with salt and pepper, then brown on all sides by turning every 1-2 minutes. Transfer to a greased slow cooker.
2. Whisk together honey, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, lime juice, minced garlic, and ginger in a bowl. Pour the mixture over the pork and cover.
3. Cook on LOW for 6-8 hours or on HIGH for 4-6 hours until the internal temperature reaches 160 degrees F.
4. Remove the pork to a serving plate and cover with foil.
5. Pour the liquid from the slow cooker into a saucepan. Whisk in cornstarch and cook over medium-high heat until thickened into a sauce.
6. Pour sauce over the pork. Garnish with cilantro and lime wedges, then serve.
