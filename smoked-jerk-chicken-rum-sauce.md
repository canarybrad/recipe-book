# Smoked Jerk Chicken with Caribbean Rum Sauce

## Ingredients

### Jerk Chicken Marinade
- 1 peeled sweet onion
- 3 green onions
- 1 scotch bonnet pepper (or 1 habanero or 2 jalapenos)
- 6 garlic cloves
- 6 fresh thyme sprigs
- 1 teaspoon peeled fresh ginger
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 1/4 cup white vinegar
- 1/2 cup olive oil
- Juice of 2 limes
- 1 tablespoon sea salt
- 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1/2 teaspoon cloves
- 3 pounds chicken thighs, drums, or breasts

### Caribbean Rum Sauce
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 3/4 cup dark rum
- 3/4 cup ketchup
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons lime juice
- 2 tablespoons jerk seasoning
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon ginger paste (or fresh grated ginger)

## Instructions

### Jerk Chicken
1. Blend all marinade ingredients (except chicken) on high speed until slightly chunky, resembling applesauce.
2. Transfer mixture to a zip bag or container and completely submerge the chicken.
3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or up to 24 hours.
4. Set up smoker at 275°F using pimento wood or fruitwood (apple or cherry) if available.
5. Remove chicken from marinade and place on smoker. Smoke for 1.5 to 2 hours until internal temperature reaches 165°F, turning occasionally.
6. Alternatively, grill at 400-450°F for about 25 minutes, turning every 8 minutes to develop light char.

### Caribbean Rum Sauce
1. Combine all sauce ingredients in a saucepan over medium-high heat.
2. Whisk together until evenly combined.
3. Once it comes to a boil, turn heat to low and simmer for 5-7 minutes, whisking occasionally.
4. Brush sauce onto chicken during the last 15 minutes of cooking, or serve alongside.
