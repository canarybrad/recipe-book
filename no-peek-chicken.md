# No Peek Chicken

## Ingredients
- 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
- 1 (10.5 oz) can cream of chicken soup
- 1 (10.5 oz) can cream of mushroom soup
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 2 cups uncooked long-grain white rice
- 1 packet onion soup mix

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Lightly spray a 9x13 inch baking dish with cooking spray.
2. Pour cream of chicken soup, cream of mushroom soup, chicken broth, rice, and onion soup mix into the baking dish. Whisk together until combined.
3. Place chicken breasts on top of the rice mixture.
4. Cover tightly with aluminum foil.
5. Bake for 1 hour 15 minutes to 1 hour 30 minutes. Do not peek or remove the foil during cooking -- the steam is what cooks the rice and keeps the chicken tender.
