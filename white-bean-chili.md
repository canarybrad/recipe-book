# White Bean Chili

## Ingredients
- 1 lb dried navy beans
- 5 cups chicken stock
- 4 tablespoons (1/2 stick) butter
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 3/4 cup diced onion
- 1 1/2 cans chopped green chilies (or jalapeno peppers)
- 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, finely chopped
- 1 tablespoon ground cumin
- 1 tablespoon dried oregano
- 1 to 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/2 bunch cilantro, chopped
- Red pepper flakes to taste

## Instructions
1. Wash beans, cover with water, and soak for 2 hours. Drain.
2. Place beans in a large pot with chicken stock and bring to a boil.
3. In a saucepan, heat butter and saute garlic, onion, and chilies for 5 minutes.
4. Add the sauteed mixture to the bean pot.
5. Add chicken, cumin, oregano, black pepper, white pepper, red pepper flakes, and cilantro.
6. Lower heat to medium and cook, stirring occasionally, for approximately 1 1/2 hours.
7. Serve with cornbread.
