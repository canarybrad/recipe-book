# Grown-Up Mac and Cheese

## Ingredients
- 1/4 pound thick-sliced bacon
- 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
- Kosher salt
- 2 cups elbow macaroni or cavatappi
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 4 ounces gruyere cheese, grated
- 3 ounces extra sharp cheddar cheese, grated
- 2 ounces blue cheese, crumbled
- 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- Pinch of ground nutmeg
- 2 slices white sandwich bread, crusts removed
- 2 tablespoons fresh basil leaves, chopped

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Arrange bacon on a baking rack set on a sheet pan. Bake 15-20 minutes until crispy. Drain on paper towels and crumble when cooled.
2. Boil salted water with oil in a large pot. Cook macaroni 6-8 minutes and drain well.
3. Warm milk in a small saucepan without boiling.
4. Melt butter in a medium pan and add flour. Cook 2 minutes while whisking constantly.
5. Gradually add hot milk while whisking. Cook until thickened.
6. Stir in gruyere, cheddar, blue cheese, salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
7. Fold in cooked macaroni and crumbled bacon.
8. Divide into 2 gratin dishes.
9. Pulse bread in a food processor to coarse crumbs, add basil and pulse again. Top pasta with breadcrumb mixture.
10. Bake 35-40 minutes until golden and bubbly.
