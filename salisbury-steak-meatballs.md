# Salisbury Steak Meatballs

## Ingredients

### Meatballs
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1/3 cup Italian style bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoons ketchup
- 2 tablespoons mustard
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- 2 large egg yolks
- Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

### Gravy and Noodles
- 8 ounces egg noodles
- 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 1 onion, thinly sliced
- 2 cups beef broth, divided (or more, to taste)
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tablespoon ketchup
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley leaves

## Instructions

1. Cook egg noodles in boiling salted water according to package directions; drain well.
2. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, bread crumbs, ketchup, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, and egg yolks. Season with salt and pepper and mix until combined. Form into approximately 20 meatballs, about 1 1/4 to 1 1/2 inches each.
3. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Brown meatballs in batches on all sides, about 4-5 minutes. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate.
4. Whisk together 1/2 cup beef broth and cornstarch in a small bowl; set aside.
5. Add sliced onions to the skillet and cook until golden brown and translucent, about 2-3 minutes. Whisk in the remaining 1 1/2 cups beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, and ketchup; cook 1-2 minutes.
6. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and gradually whisk in the cornstarch mixture. Return meatballs to the skillet and cook until meatballs are cooked through and sauce has thickened, about 3-4 minutes. Add more broth as needed for desired consistency.
7. Serve immediately over egg noodles, garnished with parsley.
