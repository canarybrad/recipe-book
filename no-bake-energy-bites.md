# No-Bake Energy Bites

## Ingredients
- 1 cup old-fashioned oats
- 2/3 cup toasted shredded coconut
- 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
- 1/2 cup ground flaxseed
- 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
- 1/3 cup honey
- 1 tablespoon chia seeds (optional)
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions
1. Stir all ingredients together in a medium bowl until thoroughly mixed.
2. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes to 1 hour so the mixture is easier to handle.
3. Roll into balls about 1 inch in diameter.
4. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
